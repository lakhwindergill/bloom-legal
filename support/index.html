Support

If you need help with Bloom, contact us at support@bloomapp.au.

Typical response time: 2–3 business days.

Before contacting support

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Log out and log back in if data is not syncing.
  • Restart the app if something looks stuck.

When reporting a bug

Please include your device model, operating system version, Bloom app version, screenshots if helpful, and the steps to reproduce the issue.

Feature requests

We welcome ideas that help parents document and support their child's journey. Send feature suggestions to support@bloomapp.au.

FAQ

Who is Bloom for?

Bloom is designed for parents and caregivers who want to privately record a child's milestones, goals, achievements, memories and growth journey.

Can children create accounts?

No. Bloom is intended for adult parents and caregivers.

How do I delete my account?

Open Bloom, go to Settings, scroll to Danger Zone, and tap Delete My Account.

Do you sell my data?

No. Bloom does not sell personal information.
