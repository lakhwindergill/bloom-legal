Delete Your Bloom Account

You can permanently delete your Bloom account directly from within the app.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the Bloom app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Delete My Account.
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, Bloom deletes or schedules deletion of data associated with your account, including:

  • Your account profile and email association.
  • Child profiles.
  • Milestones.
  • Goals.
  • Journal entries and memories.
  • Achievements.
  • Photos and uploaded content, where applicable.
  • Other app data linked to your account.

Data that may be retained

Some limited information may be retained where required by law, security obligations, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, backup recovery, or legitimate operational needs. Retained information is handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need assistance deleting your account, email support@bloomapp.au from the email address linked to your Bloom account.
