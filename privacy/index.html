Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Bloom helps parents and caregivers record child development information, memories, milestones, goals and achievements. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how users can control or delete their information.

Contact: support@bloomapp.au

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, password authentication metadata and user ID.
  • Child profile information entered by the user, including names, dates, notes, milestones, goals, achievements and memories.
  • Photos or other user-generated content uploaded by the user, where available in the app.
  • Technical data such as device type, app version, crash logs, diagnostics and security logs when enabled.

How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To provide Bloom's core app functionality.
  • To store, display and sync user-created child development records.
  • To improve reliability, security and performance.
  • To respond to support requests and account deletion requests.

Storage and service providers

Bloom may use third-party infrastructure providers such as Supabase, cloud databases, authentication services, cloud storage, analytics or crash reporting tools. These providers process data on our behalf to operate the app. We do not sell personal information.

Children's information

Bloom is intended for use by parents and caregivers. Children should not create accounts or use Bloom independently. Parents and caregivers are responsible for the information they choose to add about children.

Data sharing

We do not share personal data with other users through a public social feed or messaging system. Data may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app, comply with law, protect rights, investigate abuse, or complete account deletion requests.

Security

Data transmitted between the app and our services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS where supported. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect user information.

Account deletion

Users can delete their account from inside the Bloom app under Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Account. See the Delete Account page for details about deletion and retention.

Retention

We retain account and app data while the account is active. When an account is deleted, associated data is deleted or de-identified unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention or legitimate operational reasons.

User rights

Users may request access, correction or deletion of their personal information by contacting support@bloomapp.au.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Bloom evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
